# ⚡ Energy Demand Forecasting

## Overview
Forecasted **energy demand and consumption** using statistical and time series models for optimization of supply chains in the energy sector.

## Objective
To help energy companies plan generation and resource allocation efficiently.

## Techniques
- **Models:** Holt’s Linear Trend, Holt-Winters Additive Seasonality  
- **Tools:** Python, Pandas, Statsmodels  
- **Visualization:** Seasonal decomposition and forecast plots  

## Results
- Holt-Winters model achieved a forecasting accuracy of 93%.  
- Demonstrated value in predicting monthly consumption patterns.

Accurate **energy forecasting** supports strategic planning in oil and gas and renewable energy sectors.
