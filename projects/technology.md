## Overview
Developed a **Tech Sales Forecasting Dashboard** to analyze and predict smartphone sales trends over time.

## Objective
To provide actionable insights for product managers and marketing teams for better inventory and pricing decisions.

## Techniques
- **Modeling:** Simple Exponential Smoothing, Holt-Winters Seasonal  
- **Tools:** Python, Power BI, Pandas, Statsmodels  
- **Metrics:** MAD (Mean Absolute Deviation), MSE (Mean Squared Error)

## Results
- Holt-Winters additive model gave best performance with MSE = 0.012.  
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize sales over 17 weeks and predict week 18.
